## Linux CLI Review
I reviewed the fundamental Linux concepts required for Day One operations. Key areas of focus included:
* **Process State Management:** Differentiating between standard processes, daemons, and zombie processes, and understanding the graceful termination of services (`SIGTERM` vs `SIGKILL`).
* **Resource Auditing:** Utilizing `df`, `du`, and `free` to rapidly diagnose system starvation.
* **Security & Permissions:** Mastering octal notation (`chmod 755`) and inode structures to ensure files are secure from unauthorized execution.
* **Network Triage:** Using tools like `netstat`, `ss`, and `curl` to debug container-to-container connectivity issues directly from the bash shell.
